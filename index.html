T2DM Health Plan Decision Aid

Using Preference-Space Latent Class Coefficients

This tool implements the latent class model from three experiments (in preference space), reflecting how individuals trade off efficacy, risk, and cost for T2DM treatments.

  • Experiment 1: Only self-focused attributes.
  • Experiment 2: Self & others in poorer health, but outcomes are equal (so there are no separate “others” parameters).
  • Experiment 3: Self vs. others in poorer health with unequal outcomes (includes separate “others” parameters).

Enter the patient's demographic profile, select an experiment, and adjust attribute sliders/inputs to see the predicted class membership and plan uptake probability versus opting out.
