T2DM Health Plan Decision Aid

Using Preference-Space Latent Class Coefficients

Instructions

Welcome to the T2DM Health Plan Decision Aid. This tool utilizes a latent class model to predict the probability of a patient choosing a specific health plan versus opting out based on their characteristics and treatment attributes.

  • Experiment 1: Only self-focused attributes are considered.
  • Experiment 2: Considers both self and others in poorer health with equal health outcomes.
  • Experiment 3: Compares self versus others in poorer health with unequal health outcomes.

To use the tool:

  1. Navigate to the Input tab.
  2. Enter the patient's demographic information.
  3. Select the relevant experiment scenario.
  4. Adjust the treatment attributes using sliders.
  5. Click the Predict Plan Uptake button.
  6. Review the results in the Results tab.

For detailed explanations and recommendations, visit the Downloads tab.
